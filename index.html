About

Hello, I’m Albert Einstein. I’ve always been fascinated by the mysteries of the universe—how time bends, how energy and matter connect, and how a simple question can change everything. Some know me for E=mc², but to me, science is much more than equations. It’s about curiosity, exploration, and never being afraid to question what we think we know. When I’m not working on theories, I enjoy pondering life, humanity, and the power of imagination—because sometimes, the biggest discoveries start with a simple “what if?”
